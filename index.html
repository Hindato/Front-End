About Us

We teach you how to provide a safe environment for the elderly at home, how to deal with emergency situations, learn how to monitor the health of the elderly, recognize signs of illness, and how to deal with medications.

Services

Psychological Support

Learn how to communicate with the elderly, provide him with emotional and psychological support, and alleviate his feelings of loneliness and depression.

Care Skills

Learn how to assist the elderly person with daily activities such as bathing, dressing, eating, and moving around. Learn how to monitor the elderly person’s health, recognize signs of illness, and how to handle medications.

Maintaining Your Health

Learn how to deal with stress and fatigue resulting from care, and how to maintain your physical and mental health
